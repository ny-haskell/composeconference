Travel & Accomodations

Subway

The subway runs at all hours and goes just about everywhere. The nearest stop is the 1 train at 18th street. There are often service disruptions on weekends, so it is worth checking the website to see if any lines are rerouted.

Train

Take Amtrak to Penn Station, which is located in Midtown Manhattan, or the Metro-North to Grand Central.

Airports

The closest major airports are John F. Kennedy International Airport (JFK), LaGuardia Airport (LGA), and Newark International Airport (EWR). All are accessible by public transportation - train, bus, shuttles.

NYC has many fine hotels and hostels. We suggest something by the 1, F, or M lines in lower Manhattan or downtown Brooklyn.
